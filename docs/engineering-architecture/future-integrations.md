# Future Integrations

## 1. POS Systems
- Integrate with retail POS systems to capture receipts and transaction metadata automatically
- Support downstream analytics for consumer and merchant workflows

## 2. Retail APIs
- Connect to product and inventory APIs for richer product matching and availability data
- Support cross-retailer comparisons and sustainability scoring

## 3. Barcode APIs
- Use barcode lookup APIs to enrich receipt item recognition
- Improve confidence and standardize product metadata

## 4. Payment Systems
- Enable receipt-linked payment and financial reconciliation workflows
- Support enterprise billing and audit integrations

## 5. Loyalty Programs
- Connect with loyalty platforms for personalized sustainability recommendations
- Enable points and rewards for lower-carbon purchases
